GoldenHacks

Wilfrid Laurier University

Waterloo, Ontario, Canada

Information For Hackers

GoldenHacks is an event where you'll collaborate together with like-minded people to solve the biggest problems in various industries! You'll be working with business students, engineering students and more!

It's like a themed hackathon, except the themes will be determined by the title sponsors, coming to you with their industry related problems.

Questions? Get in touch with us, we don't bite :)

Important Information:

Date: March 2019

Location: Wilfrid Laurier University; 75 University Ave W, Waterloo, ON N2L 3C5 (map below)

Information For Sponsors

Want to meet and work with bright students looking to create positive change to the industry? Want to contribute to one of the fastest growing start-up communities in the world? Seeking exposure of your company? GoldenHacks is looking for sponsors like you!

Title sponsors get to pitch problems to the attendants, similar to a case competition. This is a perfect opportunity to get solutions to your biggest problems. Take a look at the Title Package for more information.

Not able to be a title sponsor? No problem. There are varying tiers of how you can sponsor, take a look at the tiers in the General Package for more information.

If you have any other questions that aren't answered by the package, please don't hesitate to contact us. We will respond to you shortly!
